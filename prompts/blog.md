## Prompt 1: Service Explainer Post
```
Write a 600-word blog post for Pet Playground, a pet services business.
Topic: What to expect when you hire us for [specific service].
Tone: Friendly, professional, reassuring.
Audience: Homeowners/clients who are researching options.
Include: A brief intro, 3-4 key points, and a call to action to get a free quote.
```
